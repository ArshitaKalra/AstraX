About Us

AstraX is the annual AstroFest organised by the Space Technology and Astronomy Cell, IIT Mandi. The fest features an amazing panel of Keynote speakers, events, workshops, all hosted in the amazing and beautiful campus of Indian Institute of Technology Mandi. Space Technology and Astronomy Cell, also STAC, IIT Mandi is the official Astronomy Club of IIT Mandi.
